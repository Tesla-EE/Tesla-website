About Tesla 2024

TESLA is not just an event;
It's a powerhouse of innovation and energy brought to you by the students of
the Electrical Engineering Department. Named after the legendary inventor Nikola Tesla, this event embodies his spirit of ingenuity and groundbreaking discoveries.
TESLA is where the brightest minds in electrical engineering converge to showcase their revolutionary ideas and projects,
setting the stage for a future powered by innovation.
With Innovative Projects Showcase, Power-Packed Workshops, Panel Discussions, High-Voltage Hackathons and many more,
TESLA aims to empower attendees with knowledge and connections to drive the future of electrical engineering.
Join TESLA and be part of the energy revolution!
